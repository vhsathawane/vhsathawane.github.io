About Me 🫡

I currently pursue my Ph.D. at IIT Bhilai within the Computer Science and Engineering department, where I am under the guidance of Prof. Rajat Moona and Prof. Santosh Biswas. My research focuses on developing a secure interoperable payment architecture for public transportation in India. Prior to this, I accumulated six years of teaching experience at esteemed institutions such as VNIT Nagpur and NIT Raipur. Throughout my career, I have conducted over 50 expert lectures and sessions in various engineering colleges.
